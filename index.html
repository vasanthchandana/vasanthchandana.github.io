How it Started ?

Our story strated in 2020, Feb 15th When I saw Chandana for first time in my brothers marriage, It was like a love at first sight. I'm generally not an outspoken guy but that day I felt like I should speak to her, and then when I spoke to her I felt "Yes, this is the one for me". I took her number but unfortunately lost it, upon that I forgot to ask her name. Later I tried to get contact through multiple ways but tough luck, I couldn't. Then I told myself if its for me It will come back to me.

After 3 years

Later in 2023, her profile was brought to me by my brothers, thats when I confirmed myself yes, we are destined to be together. I personally met her on 6th of May, and took her opinion. Within a month our families met, and Finally we got engaged and marriage dates got fixed.
